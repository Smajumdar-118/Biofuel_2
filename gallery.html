About

It is possible to create biofuel from coffee waste without extracting the oil from spent coffee grounds. One method involves using a process called pyrolysis, which involves heating the coffee waste to high temperatures in the absence of oxygen. This process breaks down the coffee waste into a liquid bio-oil that can be used as a fuel.
